Quezon City Sharks Promotion Poster

January 2023 - February 2023

Summary

The Quezon City project was my grade 12 Media Arts year long assignment, where we were responsible for creating the basic foundations of creating a brand. The logo was created using illustrator and is commonly used throughout the mini projects within the Quezon City collection as the main focal point.

I have designed here a game day poster for the historic night of the Sharks’ Home Opener. The poster consists of a sunset background to match the team logo colours and the habitat of the shark. I also have eye catching orange text boxes filled with game information to attract the consumer’s eye. The poster has our team slogan in addition to action photos of what fans should expect to see at the game.

Tools Used

  • Illustrator
  • Photoshop
