Quezon City Sharks Business Cards

November 2023 - December 2023

Summary

The Quezon City project was my grade 12 Media Arts year long assignment, where we were responsible for creating the basic foundations of creating a brand. The logo was created using illustrator and is commonly used throughout the mini projects within the Quezon City collection as the main focal point.

In this mini project, I crafted both the front and back designs of the Quezon City Sharks' Business Cards. To honor the team's hometown, I incorporated the Quezon City skyline into my design. On the front, I featured the team slogan in a medium-sized, straightforward graffiti font, ensuring it complements rather than overshadows our logo. The back of the card includes vital contact details such as phone numbers, websites, emails, and social media account handles.

Tools Used

  • Photoshop
  • Illustrator
  • Indesign
