Sushi Me Roll'n Food Truck

April 2022 - June 2022

Summary

Sushi Me Roll'n was assigned as the culmination of my Grade 11 Media Arts class. Crafted with meticulous attention to detail, the food truck's design was crafted on Photoshop. The distinctive logo, embodying the face of our brand, was brought to life using Illustrator, while the menu, a cornerstone of our culinary narrative, found its form on InDesign.

The name, "Sushi Me Roll'n," serves as a playful twist on the lyrics "you see me rollin", and cleverly implemented into our logo in a striking, attention-grabbing font. Drawing inspiration from a colour palette aimed at evoking warmth and friendliness, vibrant shades of red, yellow, black, white, and hints of green adorn the truck, resonating with a sense of excitement and approachability.

Seen all around the truck are the cartoon sushi figures, strategically placed intended to entice viewers to explore the food we have to offer. Each element are meticulously selected and thoughtfully integrated to our design with hopes to invite viewers to take a stroll at Sushi Me Roll'n

Tools Used

  • Illustrator
  • Photoshop
  • Indesign
