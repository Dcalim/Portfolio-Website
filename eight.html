Quezon City Sharks Website

April 2023 - June 2023

Summary

The Quezon City project was my grade 12 Media Arts year long assignment, where we were responsible for creating the basic foundations of creating a brand. The logo was created using illustrator and is commonly used throughout the mini projects within the Quezon City collection as the main focal point.

Through WIX, I crafted the Quezon City Sharks Website. The process of developing this platform enlightened me on fundamental User Experience principles, particularly the importance of making essential information readily accessible on the home page. To achieve this, I integrated a search bar, drop-down menus, and a cart icon displaying the number of items in the user's cart. These features streamline navigation and enhance user engagement with the site.

Tools Used

  • WIX
  • Photoshop
