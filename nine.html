Hoops

June 2023 - July 2023

Summary

This project was something I created over the summer of my transition to university. The process of creating this game was quite fun as I never had any experience of implementing a user inteface into a game.

I developed a game called Hoops using Processing. It features a basketball that jumps towards the direction of your mouse upon a mouse click and free falls when no click is detected. The objective of the game is to score as many baskets as possible before the time limit expires. Additionally, pink time balls appear throughout the game; when your ball hits one, it adds more time, providing you with extra opportunities to surpass your high score.
